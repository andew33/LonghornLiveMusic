Austin Music Information

Austin is known as the "Live Music Capital of the World", with many live performances held annually around the city.
Austin has multiple world-renowned venues that hosts both international and local artists, such as South by Southwest (SXSW) and Austin City Limits (ACL).
You can check out live music around unexpected places in Austin as well, like in grocery stores and the Austin-Bergstrom Int'l Airport.
Some recognized musicians that hail from Austin include country rockstar Willie Nelson and R&B guitarist Gary Clark Jr.
Many local artists and bands have their roots in Austin's local venues, making our city a welcoming place for anyone interested in checking out unique sound.
